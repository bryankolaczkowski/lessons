BACKGROUND PREREQUISITES

a touch of programming, statistics or AI... but not all three

What do I need to know before taking this course?

You need a tiny bit of either
  • computer programming
  • statistics
  • artificial intelligence

Artificial intelligence (AI) exists at the interface of computer programming and statistics; any experience in either of these fields will be helpful in this course. If you have taken an introduction to programming course (in college, high-school or online), you will have an easier time with the computer programming content in this course. If you have taken an introduction to statistics course, you will have an easier time with the basic statistics content. If you have taken an introduction to AI course, you will have an easier time with the AI content.

You will probably struggle unnecessarily if you have no previous exposure to any of these topics. But so long as you have at least some minimal experience in at least one of these areas, you should be well-prepared for the course material.

Is this course 'easy'? Well, I hope not. I hope this course will be challenging, fun and rewarding! In this case, I hope it is all three, rather than at least one!

What will I learn in this course?

You will learn how to use artificial intelligence (AI) like a pro.

This course teaches you how to design, build and apply neural networks for data analysis, particularly focused on applications in the agricultural and life sciences. This is a 'hands on' course; you will develop, train and evaluate your own AI systems, and you will use your AI to analyze real data.

In addition to learning how to program AIs, you will learn what a modern-day AI is, how it works, and how to apply 'best practices' during AI development, training and evaluation, to make sure your AI is working the way it should.

This course uses the same tools and techniques used by AI professionals in a wide variety of fields in both industry and research. We will use tensorflow as our AI platform. Originally developed by google, tensorflow is a high-performance AI library that is among the most popular libraries used by industry and research professionals today.

Although R has reigned supreme as a statistical programming language, in AI Python is definitely king. So we'll be using the Python language to program our AIs. Don't worry if you lack experience with Python, or experience in computer programming in general. We'll teach you what you need to know to program an AI, and the tensorflow library makes AI programming very simple, at least for the 'standard' types of AIs used to solve most problems.
